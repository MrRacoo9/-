WAFERS IN 20 MINUTES

recipe of tasty, crispy and soft waffles

Ingredients:

  • Chicken egg - 3 pcs. (medium size)
  • Sugar - 100 g
  • Milk - 250 ml (2.5% fat)
  • Butter - 200 g (72% fat)
  • Wheat flour - 350 g
  • Lemon juice - 1 tbsp.
  • Baking powder - 2 tsp (without slide)
    •

    Cooking:

    1. For the dough, you need to take oil at room temperature.
      2. It is best if you take it out of the refrigerator in advance.
      I wouldn't recommend warming cold oil in the microwave.
      for part of it will quickly melt, while the other remains cold and hard.

    2. Soft butter should be rubbed with sugar. You can use a fork
      but why spend a lot of effort if there is a mixer.
      Whisk butter and sugar at low speed until smooth and fluffy.

    3. Beat eggs. I have an average size of eggs, about 65 g with a shell.
      Again, at low speed of the mixer, beat the dough.

    4. Pour milk into the dough. It should be fresh, not sour, and preferably not too cold.
      Whisk again. Try to do everything at low speeds,
      so that the mass does not scatter from the bowl.

    5. Squeeze the juice into a tablespoon and pour into the dough.

    6. Sift the required amount of flour there with the addition of baking powder.
      7. Stir with either a spoon or the whisk of a mixer.

    7. The dough for this recipe should be smooth, uniform,
      as thick as pancakes.

    8. Grease the waffle iron with a drop of vegetable oil with a culinary brush,
      warm up the appliance well.


    9. Put the dough in the middle of the mold and close the waffle iron tightly. One waffle requires approximately 2 tablespoons of dough.


    10. Bake Viennese waffles for about 5 minutes, but the baking time for each model of waffle iron will be different. Therefore, control the browning of the waffles. My waffles took about 5 minutes to bake.
      From this amount of dough, I prepared 10 wafers measuring 10 * 10 cm.



      11. Let me share one trick.. Immediately after baking, the waffles are crispy on the outside and soft on the inside.
      But as soon as they lie down for a little more than an hour, the waffles become soft,
      and that delicious crispy crust is gone.
      To keep waffles crispy, I recommend putting them on a wire rack before serving.
      (can be on a baking sheet) and dry in the oven at a temperature of 160 degrees for 4-5 minutes.
      Such waffles will be crispy again, even on the second day.

    11. Serve them warm - it tastes so much better. But they are good cold too.
      There are quite a few ways to submit.
